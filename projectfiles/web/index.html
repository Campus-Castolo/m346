About Us

Doupont Holdings LLC, originally founded as Mille-Feuille in Geneva during the Great Depression by Frédéric Doupont, evolved from a pastry vendor into a finance-focused subsidiary under Éric Doupont's leadership in New York City.
In 1950, the company established a branch in Geneva, becoming a pivotal hub for international business expansion.
In the 1970s, Doupont Holdings strategically acquired Techtronics Inc. in Silicon Valley, GreenGro Farms in agribusiness, and EcoPower Systems in renewable energy, diversifying its portfolio and adapting to emerging industries.
For their 80th anniversary in 1980, Doupont Holdings acquired EuroStock Traders Ltd. in London, solidifying their presence in the European financial markets.
Throughout its history, Doupont Holdings has maintained a tribute to its humble pastry origins with the Mille-Feuille patisserie in Geneva, while expanding its global influence in finance, technology, agriculture, and renewable energy.
